Shipping from Miami & Fort Lauderdale (Port Everglades)

South Florida is the #1 U.S. gateway for Caribbean trade. Stella Line arranges containers, break-bulk and project cargo from Miami and Fort Lauderdale to the Dominican Republic, Venezuela, Colombia and across the Caribbean.

Port Everglades Miami

The #1 U.S. gateway to the Caribbean

Port Everglades in Fort Lauderdale is the leading U.S. port for Caribbean trade, directing roughly 90% of its container volume to and from Latin America and the Caribbean. Combined with the Port of Miami, South Florida offers the shortest transit times and the widest service network in the region — and Stella Line works these lanes every week.
