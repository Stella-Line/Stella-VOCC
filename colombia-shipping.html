Ports we serve in Colombia

Cartagena

Colombia's premier container and transshipment port on the Caribbean coast.

Barranquilla

Key river port for break-bulk, bulk and project cargo at the mouth of the Magdalena.

Common origins include Houston and the U.S. Gulf, Miami and Fort Lauderdale (Port Everglades), the U.S. North East, and Mexico (Altamira, Veracruz), with typical transit of 7–12 days.
